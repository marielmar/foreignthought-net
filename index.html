Background

The person behind the work

My name is Mariel (pronounced MAH-ree-el). I live in Maryland with my husband.

I'm a Copywriter and Content Strategist who writes to make ideas click. For me, clarity isn't a style choice; it's the whole point. A curious mind and a love of learning have taken me from advertising agencies to fintech, and I've picked up a lot along the way.

Outside of work, I'm usually reading, watching films, or thinking about something I read or watched. I love spending time with family and friends. I'll hike, camp, or head to the beach every now and then, but honestly, I'm a city person. Urban landscapes, architecture, and museums are more my speed.

Before Baltimore, I lived in Mexico City. That city shaped a lot of who I am, and probably explains why I gravitate toward work that's a little intelligent, a little bold, and always useful.
